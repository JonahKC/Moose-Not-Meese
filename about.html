About Us

Founding

In 2019, our founder Jonah was surrounded by friends who didn't know how to properly plural moose. Feeling inspired, he took action. He did some research to prove that the plural of moose is moose, and then built a website and made anti-meese pins to spread awareness. Ever since, we've maintained one goal: educate people on the correct plural of moose.

Our Work

We continue working to spread awareness to this day. We frequently give out stickers, stage protests, and simply tell people the correct plural. We worked with JCWYT to build a Discord bot that deletes the word meese. We sell anti-meese shirts, stickers, and more anti-meese merch for people who want to spread the word themselves. Meesers are still a major societal issue today, and we're working to fix that.
